Chicago West Nile Project

This project started with data from a Kaggle competition. The first half of this project required my team to predict when and where specific mosquito species will test positive for the West Nile Virus. The second half of the project was to develop a treatment plan for the city of Chicago, as well as a cost-benefit analysis of that program.
