Iowa Liquor Sales Project

In this project, I received liquor sales data for the calendar year 2015 as well as the first quarter of 2016. The prompt: using the provided data, project the entirety of 2016 liquor sales across the state of Iowa, and use that information to make recommendations to the Iowa State Liquor Board and Iowa legislature on prospective measures to increase the liquor tax.

Linear regression and train/test splits were the order of the day here, after some spirited data cleaning and organization. I engineered a few features, including bottles sold per county, and trained my model on the 2015 data. I then projected forward using the Q1 2016 sales data to arrive at a projected 2016 total sales figure.
