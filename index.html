Who's This Guy?

Hi! My name is James. I'm a data scientist with a background in physics and teaching, as well as the start-up and engineering worlds. And, as of the start of 2018, I am an alumnus of the General Assembly Data Science Immersive (Washington, DC).

My style of data science, and problem solving in general, is best described as tenacious. When a stubborn problem crosses my path, I go to great lengths to find a creative solution - usually by being more stubborn than the problem!

My main data science interests include data cleaning, data architecture, regression and predictive modeling, clustering, and machine learning. My main interests outside the data science arena? Football, public transportation, reading, and weightlifting.
